Do you want to know my Learning Journey? Come, let's look through together

Learning in a Changing World has brought on a number of positive learning experiences. I have started my learning journey with holding my parent's hand in 2003 when I was 2.5 years old. From begining there was many ups and downs but I had two strong weapon equipped in me which are down to earth behaviour and my never give up attitude.

I have started schooling from Burdwan Municipal High School. In my school days i was a average student. In my early days I was a little bit introvert that I don't like talk too much. At that time my teachers could hardly recognize me. After promoted to the high school I have joined the school BasketBall team, In one year of training I got selected to the West Bengal Junior BasketBall Team. After Coming from the tour suddenly I became popular in the school, then every teacher could recognise me not only for my good behaviour but also for my skills. From my childhood I was in love with solving the problems in Math. I had appeared in my secondary board exam in the year 2016 and got 97 in Math & the total of 84% in the examination. And after 2 years in 2018 I have passed my higher secondary examination with 94 in Math and my total was 77.80%. Thus began my college journey.

I am pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Haldia Institute of Technology. Like other guys I was also very curious in my 1st year of my college days. Now I am in 3rd year. In my 1st year I have started basics programming and the starting was not really hard for me as I was familiar with it from my high school. I have learnt C and Python Language in my 1st year of college days. In my 2nd year I have started working on Web Development using HTML, CSS & JavaScript. Now at the last of my 3rd year I know C, C++, Python, Java programming languages.
