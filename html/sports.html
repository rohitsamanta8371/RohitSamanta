Do you want to know my Sports Journey? Come, let's look through together

Sports are an important part of just about every society and country involving everyone, whether they are playing or watching. Sports often reflect the culture of the countries in which they are played. In many ways, sports define a society. From my childhood I love to play various Sports and Games. I am from a conservative family in my early days i neverallowed to play outside.

When I was in 7th standard of schooling I have joined school BasketBall team as a trainee. In begining I would get tired easily as I love fastfood very much. Then gradually I stopped eating fastfood. My coach helped me a lot in this phase, he was less a coach than a elder brother to me. After 4 months of joining as a trainee I got selected in the school basketball team for West Bengal Inter School Championship which was held in kolkata sponsored by RISE Worldwide formerly known as IMG Relaiance Limited. In this tournament hardly got the chance to went down to the field, when any of the senior player get enjoured or absent then I got the chance. Unfortunately we lost the tournament, likewise my journey began. After returning from the tour I practice hard and my coach is also helped me a lot.

After few months in District Junior BasketBall Tournament Our school team finally became the winner in my captionship, That was really a proud moment for me and having such supportive fellow friends. From my school I was selected for the Burdwan District Basketball Team. After few the Inter District Basketball Tournament held on kolkata, and we got the Runners up position.

After few weaks of returning from the tour I got a letter, i was really surprised opening the letter and seeing "You got selected in West Bengal National BasketBall Team." After that I got 2 bestplayer award in 2 consecutive years. And played for 5 club teams. I was totally detached in the time of my higher secondary exam. After joining the college again I have stated my practice daily basis and got the best shooter and one man of the match prize. Now I am totally out of practice for the lockdown. Waiting for the coronavirus pandamic to end.
